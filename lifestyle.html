LIFESTYLE

WAY OF LIFE

Some people say eating healthy and working out is a lifestyle. Lots of people that commit to a healthy diet decide that it's going to be their way of life. Eating healthier makes them feel better which in turn makes them continue. Switching to this lifestyle can improve how you see yourself, boost metabolism, lose weight, strengthen your immune system, and help fight off disease.

Steps to start

Some step sto start out with are managing your workout, food plan, stress, and sleep. All these components work together to make you feel better.

Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle can be a big change for some poeple. Try starting small and working your way up. As you feel more comfortable try new things.

Move More

Instead of spending your sunday on the couch go on a hike or go on a walk with the dog. Keep busy as much as you can, joining classes like Zumba or belly dancing are good ways to move throughout your day. Also keep track of how much you move, it'll help motivate you.

Diet

Changing your diet to be packed full of more nutrients and fresher is a great step. Come up with food plans for the week to help you keep a schedule. Also throw out the guilt of slipping up. When you give into a cooke, just work a little harder at the gym tomorrow.

Manage Stress

Finding ways to manage or reduce your stress makes you healthier. Develop positive coping skills like meditation and visualization. Help other out more, it'll make you feel better. Another good tip is to keep a journal about your feelings so you can reflect and improve. And remember to breathe.

Sleep Better

Sleep is very important part of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Setting a sleep schedule can help you get the extra Zzzs you need. Also try not to watch tv or use the computer right before you sleep. The light makes your body sense that its time to be awake.

TIPS

Here are some tips about how to maintain a healthy life style.

Portions

Portion control your meals. By doing this you are tracking your calories and making sure not to over eat.

Change

Mix up your routine, both workout and diet. Itll help keep you interested.

Meal Time

Remember to eat at every meal. Starving yourself won't do any good for your body. Choose wisely for snacks, almonds are a good midday booster.

Meal Plan

Make a meal plan so you don't end up eating out on a busy night. Cooking ahead of time or knowing what you're going to eat helps maintain your plan.

Teamwork

Have your family and friends join in on your new habits. It's helpful to have the people around you eating well too.

A Better You!

Don't give up, remember it's all going to improve your life.
